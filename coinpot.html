CoinPot is a faucets hub and a digital wallet similar to FaucetHub. Creating an account with CoinPot you is a prerequisite for claiming for 7 CoinPod faucets: Moon Bitcoin, Moon Dogecoin, Moon LiteCoin, Bonus Bitcoin, Bit Fun, Moon Dash and Moon BCH. When you claim from these faucets the tokens appear directly in your CoinPot Dashboard, and later you can withdraw your tokens to any other wallet you choose. The minimum withdraw from CoinPot is 0.00010000 BTC.

While claiming from CoinPd faucets can be tedious, due to a smaller reward and a high frequency (most of the faucets allow for a 5 min claim interval), the consistent claiming can qualify for additional rewards from CoinPod via the daily, weekly and yearly challanges. For instance, 750 faucet claims in a day will be rewarded with additional 500 tokens and the person who makes the most claims per month earns additional 10,000,000 tokens.
